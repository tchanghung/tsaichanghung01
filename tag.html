SIMPLE but RICH

"Delight your coffee time with more inpiration." - TOUCH AND GO COFFEE

Project Overview

2020.01~2020.04
Objective
Rich the experience of purchasing banners and arranging the lable with more interacitive and delightful user interface.
Deliverables
To reponse needs from our client and end-user, I designed a new label page with a intuitive user interface that inspires users when they tailor a original bottle for themselves. In addtion, support users smoothly go forward to explore more ideas on a delightful banner shop.
Chanllenge
Somehow there have already rather specific requirements (=limitations) from our clients, as it implied that how to visualize the concepts properly was quite important in this project.
Outcome
We created some new experience with more enjoyable elements that beyond our clien't requirements and, as we expected, TOUCH AND GO COFFEE went viral (ツイッターでバズった) again after thess new features released.

Background

Since Suntotry released α totally brand new service, TOUCH AND GO COFFEE, which based on our mobile ordering system from 2019/06, it became a big trend among teenagers, especially those who enthusiastic about the special activity in Tapan ”推し活”. Within this context, Suntory decided to conduct a customer-drawing tactic: Rich the cunstomize of the key value of this serived "Label" by 1) Enlarging the variety and quantity of banners, incluing paid-banner. 2) Providing the long-awaited feature: arrange tailor the "Label".

Define

Based on the existing elegant visual style, I decided to create a simple but enjoyable interface to keep not only the perception of the brand also the sensation.


購入体験を "Customer but FAST" から、さらに "SIMPLE but RICH" へ進化

Visible and Predictable. We assumpt that users visit the My Label page for seeking a awesome Label design that can represent his/her feeling at a certain present. Easy to adjust and always display the lastest design are necessary.

Delightful and Communicative. Inspiration and ideas always come from visual informaiton (stimuli).

ideation

I conducted the design works with several iterations to make sure whether all the hypotheses followed the design principles or not. Here are some deliverables:

簡単に編集・管理・閲覧できるマイラベルページ

  • Defined the top area as a label preview section, and user can edit the content below by a natural eye gazing path.
  • To add some slight gamification elements, I provided a random (”おまかせ”) banner for those who want to chosen by chance. And it is the default choice as well.
  • On small display (like iPhone SE), I placed a noticeable button to nudge users apply the current design once they edited some element in the existing one.

楽しい・しずる感のあるプレビュー表現

  • According to the the definition above, visible and predictable are the principles while purchasing since users are eagar to confirm the label and banner design immediately instead of waiting till pickup.
  • I created a enjoyable preview page that users not only can enjoy the animation while confirming what does it like if mapping on the real bottle without just imagine, also can select whichever banner they want to confirm immediately by easily tapping the target banner icon or swiping the bottles.

Deliver

After 3 months of development, this long-awaited features release 🎉



As our expected, TOUCH AND GO COFFEE went viral immediately when the news was pushed on the LINE official account.

Impact. Although there were just few paid banners except for the orginal ones in the early stage of this release, but it has been defintely impact on leading to more purchasing.

The trend of sales during the first month. We found some realities:

  • 95% of shoppers purchased after the regular push of banner release notificaiton on Sunday night within 24hours, and the sales of both coffee and banner were dramatically increased.
  • 80% of shoppers bought more than one set (normally there are 5 pieces) when they purchased.
